Non Profitable Organisation

The Endaevour Welfare Society is a non profitable organisation.We work for welfare in the education field.Helping vulnerable students and providing them right path is our aim.

Education is our moto

Our one and only goal is to deliver resources to everyone who wants to study,as everyone has the right to get educated.We work so that none of the children should be departed from right to education .

How we work?

We support children through unique ideas like forming study groups and child tracking system,holding workshops to create awareness among parents and providing apprenticeship oppurtunitie to students in technical courses.
